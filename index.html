Chung Yen Li
Interface Designer and Post-Rock Thinker
Bio
Hi, I am Chung Yen. I love learning and music because I trust knowledge can bring power to people and music can make people resilient. I play bass for more than ten years with little achievement, but I really release from anxiety and distress when I play it.

In the past, I intend to make people more happier through music. However, now I am more ambitious than that--I trust the world can be improved through shaping human behavior by human-computer interaction.

I have a background in electrical engineering, but have spent most of my time explore various fields. In the industry, I specialize in data visualization, prototyping and interface design.

Recently I start a new habit, rock climbing.
