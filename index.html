About
Hi, I'm Chung-Yen, a Taiwanese born design/engineer. I love learning and music. I trust knowledge can bring power to people and music can make people resilient. I play bass for more than ten years, and I release from anxiety and distress when I play it.

In the past, I intend to make people happier through music, and now I am more ambitious than that. I trust the world can be improved through improving human-computer interaction.

I have a background in electrical engineering, but have spent most of my time explore various fields. In the industry, I specialize in data visualization, prototyping, and interface design.

I love sports too, and recently I start a new habit-rock climbing.
